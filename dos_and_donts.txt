To Avoid COVID-19:

Do's:

1. Wear masks in public.

2. Wash hands frequently.

3. Use hand sanitizer.

4. Maintain 6-feet distance.

5. Stay home if sick.

6. Cover coughs and sneezes.

7. Ventilate indoor spaces.

8. Get vaccinated.


Don'ts:

1. Avoid crowded places.

2. Minimize close contact.

3. Don't touch face.

4. Avoid sharing items.

5. Take symptoms seriously.

6. Limit unnecessary travel.

Following these guidelines 
reduces COVID-19 risk.
Stay informed and follow 
local health authorities' advice.
